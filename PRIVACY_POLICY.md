Privacy Policy for Fajr Buddy & Alarm Buddy

Last updated: December 8, 2025

Fajr Buddy ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Android application uses your data.

1. Data Collection

Fajr Buddy does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal user data to external servers. All data (such as alarm times, settings, and preferences) is stored locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences and is never shared with third parties.

2. Permissions & Usage

Our app requires specific permissions to function as an alarm clock:

NFC (Near Field Communication): Used strictly to scan NFC tags to dismiss alarms. No tag data is collected, tracking, or shared.

Overlay (Display over other apps): Used solely to display the full-screen alarm interface when the device is locked or another app is open.

Wakelock & Exact Alarm: Used to ensure the alarm rings at the precise time you set, regardless of battery saving modes.

Vibration: Used for haptic feedback and alarm vibration.
